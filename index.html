Theater Education

CSSSA 2015. At California State Summer School for the Arts, I was exposed to a college-level, intensive, theater training program. Students were required to participate in classes of Tai Chi Chuan, Movement, Core Acting, Physical Comedy, Stage Combat, Voice, and Contact Improvisation in addition to watching live theater multiple times a week.

In addition to these daily classes, I was exposed to a huge amount of art and culture. I worked with not just the nation's best student thespians, but the nation's most talented student musicians, visual artists, creative writers, dancers, and filmmakers. It was truly a life-changing experience, and I owe much of who I am today to it.
